FIKA – Climate Risk Insurance for Delivery Workers


🚀 Project Overview
FIKA is a parametric micro insurance platform designed for gig economy delivery workers. It automatically compensates workers when extreme environmental conditions such as heavy rainfall, extreme heat, or dangerous air pollution affect their ability to earn.

Unlike traditional insurance, FIKA eliminates manual claims and long waiting periods by using real time environmental data and AI driven income estimation to trigger instant payouts.

🛠 Problem Statement
Gig workers face significant financial uncertainty due to:


Weather Disruptions: No protection against income loss during floods or heatwaves.


Complex Processes: Traditional insurance claim processes are too slow and complex for the gig economy.


Affordability: A lack of low cost insurance options tailored for delivery riders.

✨ Our Solution: Parametric Insurance
FIKA introduces a model where payouts are triggered automatically based on predefined environmental thresholds.

Key Principles:

Automatic Payout: No paperwork or claim filing required.


AI-Driven: Fair compensation based on historical earnings.


Affordable: Low cost weekly premiums.

📈 Weekly Premium Model
FIKA utilizes a risk pooling sustainability model:


Premium: ₹30 to ₹50 per week via UPI autopay.


Allocation: 65% for Worker Payouts, 15% for Insurance Partners, 10% for Operations, and 10% for Platform Revenue.

🤖 AI/ML Integration
We leverage AI/ML to ensure the system is fair and secure:


Income Prediction Model: Analyzes 8-12 weeks of earnings and location demand to estimate fair compensation.


Fraud Detection Model: Monitors for fake GPS locations, multiple accounts, and intentional inactivity during weather events.


Risk Forecasting: Predicts high risk zones using historical environmental data.

📱 Application Workflow & Persona
Persona: Rahul (Delivery Rider)

Scenario: A city wide flood reduces Rahul's weekly earnings from ₹6000 to ₹3000.

System Response: The Weather API detects the threshold breach. The system identifies Rahul's zone, calculates his income gap via AI, and automatically transfers compensation to his UPI account within 24–48 hours.

Step-by-Step Flow:

Registration: Worker signs up with UPI and platform details.


Subscription: Weekly premium via UPI Autopay.


Monitoring: Real-time tracking of Rainfall (>80mm), Temperature, and AQI.


Verification: GPS and work history check to prevent fraud.


Payout: Funds sent automatically to bank/UPI.

🏗 Tech Stack

Frontend: React Native / Flutter (Mobile App), Next.js (Web Dashboard).


Backend: Node.js / FastAPI.


Database: PostgreSQL.


AI/ML: Python, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow.


APIs: Weather API, AQI Monitoring, Maps, Razorpay/UPI.


Cloud: AWS / Google Cloud Platform.
